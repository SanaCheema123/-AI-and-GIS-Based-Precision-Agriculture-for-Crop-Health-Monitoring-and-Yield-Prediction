AI and GIS-Based Precision Agriculture Pipeline

1. Collect crop yield, soil, weather, and vegetation data.
2. Clean missing and duplicate records.
3. Create crop health labels from NDVI, soil moisture, and environmental features.
4. Train yield prediction regression models.
5. Train crop health classification models.
6. Save best models.
7. Deploy models in Streamlit dashboard.
8. Visualize GIS-style maps, charts, predictions, and reports.
